FEATURED PROJECTS

Google Stock Prediction

Implementation of LSTM based Recurrent Neural Networks to predict the Stock Prices of Google based on data obtained via Kaggle. The model has 4 LSTM layers followed by Dense layers and about 46 Mn trainable parameters.

The model achieves a Mean Absolute Error of 0.028 and predicts the prices very well. The visualization for the actual and predicted values are also included with the code.
